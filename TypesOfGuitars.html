Types of guitars

There are different type of guitars but they can be classified into two types which are acoustic and electric

  • Acoustic
    • Classical Guitars

      • Classical guitars have nylon strings that are tied with a loop at the bridge. Its strings are
      thicker and the guitar produces a more mellow, softer sound than the steel-stringed guitar.

    • Steel-stringed Guitars

      • Steel-stringed guitars have steel strings that are held by pegs at the bridge. This guitar is
      also a very beginner friendly guitar.

  • Electric
    • Standard Electric Guitars

      • Electric guitars make sound by vibrating strings over a pickup the converts the vibration
      into electrical signals

    • Semi-acoustic Guitars

      • Semi-acoustic guitars have both a sound box and one or more electric pickups

    • Bass Guitars

      • Bass guitars have only 4 strings and are very long and heavy
